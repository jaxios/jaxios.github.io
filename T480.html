Fix Youtube screen tearing on Firefox

Create/edit:

/usr/share/X11/xorg.conf.d/20-intel.conf

And add:

Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Graphics"
Driver "intel"
Option "TearFree" "true"
EndSection
